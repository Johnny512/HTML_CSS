Bugs Bunny is a beloved animated character created by Warner Bros. He is best known for his starring roles in the Looney Tunes and Merrie Melodies series of animated short films. Bugs is a clever, quick-witted rabbit who often outsmarts his adversaries, including Elmer Fudd, Daffy Duck, and Yosemite Sam. With his iconic catchphrase, "What's up, Doc?", Bugs Bunny has become a cultural icon and a favorite among audiences of all ages.
